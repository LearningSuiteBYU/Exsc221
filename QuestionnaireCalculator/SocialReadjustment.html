Social Readjustment Rating Scale1

Mark each life event that has happened to you during the previous year. Total these associated points. There are no right or wrong answers. The aim is to identify which of these events you have experienced lately and to determine your risk of illness based on these events.

Life Event Life Change Unit

Score of 300+: At risk of illness.

Score of 150–299: Risk of illness is moderate

Score 0–150: Only have a slight risk of illness

1. Miller, M. A. and R. H. Rahe. “Life changes scaling for the 1990s.” Journal of Psychosomatic Research 43 (1997): 279–292.
